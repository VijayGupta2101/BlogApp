Frequently Asked Questions ⚡

Everything you need to know about logging proof of work and sharing developer blogs on WriteNest.

What exactly is "Proof of Work" (PoW)? +

Unlike traditional resumes that just summarize lists of technologies you claim to know, Proof of Work documents *what you have actually built*, complete with code snippets, deployment links, architectural summaries, and explanation of challenges you solved along the way.

Is this app connected to a real database? +

Yes! The application connects directly to a Node/Express backend that saves, retrieves, updates, and deletes records in a local SQLite file (`blog.db`). All CRUD actions are fully operational and sync instantly in real-time.

How do claps work? +

Claps are an interactive engagement feature. They are stored locally in the browser's `localStorage` for quick response and persistence, allowing you to vote and applaud your favorite posts without page refreshes.

Can I customize my dashboard tag categories? +

Absolutely. While creating or editing blogs, you can input content detailing engineering, design, blockchain, backend, or careers, and the application dynamically assigns visual tags to match the content category.
